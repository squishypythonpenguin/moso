About Moso

The best way to learn venture
is to do venture.

We build thoughtful connections between bold founders and sharp investors — acting as a trusted first touchpoint and long-term partner in the venture process.

By mirroring an investment fund, we empower top undergraduates to gain hands-on investing experience while supporting our venture and angel investors in sourcing deals and conducting diligence.

Who We Are

Penn's premier experiential VC program.

At Moso Capital, we train the next generation of investors—equipping them with real-world experience, hands-on guidance, and a network of industry leaders. Whether we're sourcing deals, conducting diligence, or working side by side with founders, we're committed collaborators—helping our venture partners uncover what's next while training future investors.

Our Commitment

A lasting partnership.

A partnership with Moso isn't just a short-term project — it's a lasting commitment to the founders we believe in and the investors we work alongside. We stay close to our portfolio long after the deal closes.

How It Works

Our process.

01
Kick-off
We start by aligning on thesis, communication cadence, and structure to ensure smooth collaboration throughout the semester with our venture and angel investors.
02
Sourcing
We tap into our unique access to Penn's networks, incubators, and investors to source standout startups worth evaluating.
03
Founder Call
We lead discovery calls with founders and loop in experts when needed to explore the business more deeply.
04
Due Diligence & Investment
We handle diligence, research, and modeling before supporting our venture partner's final investment decision.
